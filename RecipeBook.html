Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: up to 4
Ingredients Quantity
chickpeas 1-15-ounce
Fresh lemon juice 1/3 cup
garlic clove, minced 1 tbs

Instruction:

  1. Peel the Chickpeas (optional but recommended): Place the drained chickpeas in a large bowl and cover them with water.
  2. Blend Ingredients: In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, lemon juice, tahini, minced garlic, cumin, salt, and two tablespoons of olive oil.
  3. Serve: Transfer the hummus to a serving dish, drizzle it with olive oil, and sprinkle with a dash of paprika and some fresh chopped parsley for a touch of color and flavor.
